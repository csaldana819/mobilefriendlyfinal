Free Lossless Audio Codec

FLAC is a open-source compressed lossless audio codec. FLAC is one of the many standard formats of high resolution audio. With great comparisons to WAV, FLAC stands out for it's ability to be loseless while being compressed. This allows for more songs to be put on a single device. Creating a enjoyable experience for the audiophile.
